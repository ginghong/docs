## Overview

Crème’s identity is calm, editorial, and quietly smart — minimal composition, clear typography, and warm textures.

## Core aesthetic

- Clear typography with modern, minimal layouts
- Circular and pie-like geometries
- Blurs, grains, and noise for a nostalgic touch
- Grounded by sharp geometry and restrained color
